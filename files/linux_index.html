Linux Mint 'Cinnamon'

Top Reccomended distro for everyone

Linux Mint is an amazing distro that I can reccomend to anyone. If you are swapping from Windows I cannot reccomend Linux Mint enough. Coming from Windows Linux Mint Will feel like home, while not sending your data to Microsoft, or shoving AI down your throat.
